#Aptitude

Allows to the users to view the list of packages and to perform package management task such as installingm upgrading, and removing packages. Actions may be performed from a visual interface or from the command-line

The big difference with apt is the visual interface

Aptiude during a package conflict will suggest a resolution than an do the jon whereas apt snot the case.

Aptitude can interactively retrieve and displays the debian changelot of all availaible offcial packages.
